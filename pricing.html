Fee Structure

INTELLECTUAL SCHOOL SYSTEM

We don’t believe in making our students memorise facts but gain knowledge. We don’t want the study to become a burden, a work for them. It’s important to combine your classroom teaching with fun, frolic and nature. And that’s exactly what we do. I won’t say that we copy US model of education, but it will be an understatement that we are not inspired by it. We teach our students to be independent. In Montessori schools, the teacher is not a boss or an employee with pre-decided notions and goals but a guide and a partner.

One thing that really sets us apart is the cost. If you go about searching for institutes with these qualities, it is sure to cost you a fortune. But we understand that many people simply can’t afford it and this is the very reason for setting up the chain of schools.
